Free Bathroom Renovation Planning Tool

How to Plan a Bathroom Renovation

Planning a bathroom renovation can be overwhelming. How do you compare different basin options? Which toilet should you choose? How much will shower fixtures cost? This free tool helps you organize everything in one place, compare prices, and make informed decisions for your bathroom remodel.

What You Can Do

Compare bathroom fixtures like basins, toilets, showers, faucets, and tiles. Track your renovation budget in real-time. Create multiple design options (budget vs premium) to see what fits your needs. Export professional PDFs to share with contractors or family members. All completely free.

Perfect for Homeowners Who Want To:

  • Compare different bathroom basin designs and prices
  • Choose between budget and premium toilet options
  • Calculate total bathroom renovation costs
  • Compare shower heads, faucets, and fixtures
  • Select tiles and see total coverage costs
  • Track bathroom accessories and fittings
  • Create professional renovation proposals
  • Share plans with contractors for quotes

Common Questions This Tool Helps Answer:

  • How much will my bathroom renovation cost?
  • What's the difference between budget and premium bathroom fixtures?
  • Which basin style fits my bathroom design?
  • How do I compare shower options?
  • What tiles should I choose for my bathroom?
  • How can I track all my bathroom renovation expenses?
  • What fixtures do I need for a complete bathroom?

For DIY Bathroom Renovations

If you're doing a DIY bathroom remodel, this tool helps you stay organized. Add all the products you're considering - basins from different brands, various toilet models, shower fixtures, tiles, and accessories. Create different scenarios to see what combination works best for your budget and design preferences.

Multi-Currency Support

Track costs in your local currency - INR (Indian Rupees), USD (US Dollars), EUR (Euros), GBP (British Pounds), AED (UAE Dirhams), and more. Perfect for international renovations or comparing products from different regions.

How It Works

  1. Add bathroom products you're considering - basins, toilets, showers, tiles, faucets
  2. Enter prices from different suppliers or stores
  3. Upload product images to visualize your choices
  4. Create different bathroom configurations (Option A vs Option B)
  5. Compare total costs side-by-side
  6. Export professional PDFs to share with contractors or get quotes
  7. Make informed decisions based on real data

No Sign-Up Required

Start planning immediately. No registration, no email required, no subscriptions. Your data stays in your browser. Export your work as PDF or backup files whenever you need. Completely private and secure.
